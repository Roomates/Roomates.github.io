Introducing Roomate

A cohesive app that allows you to have your own messaging thread, allows for assigned chores/reminders & makes real time payments easier between you & your roommates.

Messaging with no distractions.

Our app allows you to get straight to the point with your rommates without any extra distractions. No status updates, no stories, just messages.

Payments just for home.

Make quick and easy payments to each other with only few taps of a button. You can request and send money to each other and the payments are all private to the household.

Efficient Reminders

Set self reminders as well as house reminders. Be efficient with all the reminders that pile up within your household.

Smart chores

We offer three different types of chores: automated, standard and self. With these three options you can be smart about how you assign chores!

Powerful grocery organizer

Know exactly what you need for next time you go shopping. Mark important items, get reminders when nearby stores, and get instant fund transfer when you pick up an item listed by your rooommates. Groceries made simple.

Confrim pickup

Help out your roomates!



Drive by a store on your way back home? Anyone can pick up items for the house, hitting confrim will transfer associated funds to your account.

Get Reminders

Never forget with RooMates!



Are you often forgetful? With our app you can set reminders for certian date or time, or you can set it to receive push notifications any time you are near a store that carries these items.

Contact Roommate

Got a question?



Not sure if they wanted the red one or the blue? If they need two or four? Or do you have an extra item you could lend to your roommates? With just one tap you can contact your roomates to take care of any additional details.
